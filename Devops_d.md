Why DevOps is important?



For very long times the development and the operations teams of any software enterprise have stayed at arm's length.

But this organisational cultural shift thanks to DevOps a lot of changes in forward-thinking enterprises.

Learning DevOps will help you master all the skills needed in order to successfully build, operate, monitor, measure, and improve the various processes in IT enterprises by better integrating development and operations.
